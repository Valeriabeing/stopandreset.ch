Stop & Meet Me

With 15 years navigating the high-pressure media and marketing industry, I have experienced burnout and the weight of modern stress firsthand. Based in Zürich, I bridge the gap between corporate high-performance and somatic intelligence, helping you move from chronic exhaustion to your untapped power.

Holistic approach

Your symptoms are the starting point, not the end of our work. I invite you into a safe, grounded space where we analyze your entire system to understand the "why" behind your struggle. This work requires your active presence and reflection; together, we address the root causes to help you transition from surviving to thriving.

Method with 3 pillars:

Functional Yoga

Using structural movement and alignment to build a resilient physical container.

Conscious Connected Breathing

This approach serves as a catalyst for the autonomic nervous system, going beyond the analytical mind to reach the subconscious and trigger a cellular reset that traditional talk therapy often cannot reach.

Somatic Therapy

Mapping how stress hides in the body to facilitate the integration of mind and body, resolving the root causes of dysregulation.
